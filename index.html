The Shining: A Journey into Madness

Posted on: August 29, 2024 by Sophie Steele

Stanley Kubrick's *The Shining* isn't just a horror film; it's a descent into madness. From the haunting score to the ominous Overlook Hotel, every aspect of the movie is designed to unsettle the viewer.

The film's genius lies in its ambiguity. Is Jack Torrance simply losing his mind, or is the hotel itself a malevolent force? Kubrick doesn't give easy answers, instead inviting viewers to explore the labyrinthine corridors of both the hotel and Jack's psyche.

The iconic performance by Jack Nicholson adds to the terror, making *The Shining* a timeless masterpiece of horror. Whether it's your first viewing or your fiftieth, this film continues to reveal new layers of dread.
